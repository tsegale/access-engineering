We have successfully delivered projects across Namibia and the region, serving mining, industrial, civil, and infrastructure clients. Below is a selection of our key project deliveries.

Structural upgrades, new platforms, access structures, and full rigging services at the Tsumeb smelter and processing facility.

Industrial · Mechanical
