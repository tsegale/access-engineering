Accreditations

Certifications &
Accreditations

Our certifications reflect our commitment to international quality, safety, and technical standards, giving clients confidence that every project is delivered to the highest benchmark.

NOSCAR 5 Star

The NOSA NOSCAR 5 Star rating is Namibia's highest occupational health, safety, and environmental management rating. We maintain this elite rating as a reflection of our unwavering commitment to zero-harm operations.

ISO 9001

ISO 9001 Quality Management System certification confirms that our processes, procedures, and quality controls consistently deliver services that meet customer and regulatory requirements.

ISO 45001:2018

ISO 45001 Occupational Health and Safety Management certification demonstrates our systematic approach to managing workplace safety, reducing hazards, and preventing accidents across all operations.

ISO 3834

ISO 3834 Welding Quality Management certification validates the quality requirements for fusion welding of metallic materials, covering our fabrication workshop and field welding operations.

ASME VIII / CEN / EN

Compliance with ASME Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code Section VIII, and European CEN/EN construction standards, enabling us to fabricate and install pressure equipment to international codes.
