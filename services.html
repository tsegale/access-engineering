What We Do

Our Services

Six Core Disciplines

Engineering Solutions
That Deliver

Access Engineering Technology CC offers a comprehensive range of engineering services across six core disciplines. Our multi-skilled teams operate across Namibia, delivering safe, high-quality, and cost-effective outcomes for our clients in mining, industrial, civil, oil & gas, and infrastructure sectors.

Civil Engineering

Our civil engineering division delivers comprehensive infrastructure solutions from initial earthworks to final handover. We operate across residential, commercial, and industrial civil projects throughout Namibia, with proven expertise in complex terrain and demanding environments.

  • Site preparation, clearing, and earthworks
  • Concrete foundations, slabs, and structures
  • Roads, drainage systems, and stormwater management
  • Residential and commercial building infrastructure
  • Water reticulation and civil utility installation
  • Retaining walls, kerbing, and paving
  • Full project management and quality assurance

Structural Engineering

From fabrication to final erection, our structural team handles the full lifecycle of steel and structural works. We have erected platforms at heights up to 30 metres and fabricate complex structural assemblies in our dedicated workshop facilities, certified to ISO 3834 welding standards.

  • Structural steel fabrication and erection
  • Platforms, walkways, and access structures up to 30m height
  • Handrails, guardrails, and safety access systems
  • Structural upgrades, repairs, and modifications
  • Roof structures, mezzanine floors, and support frames
  • Workshop and in-situ structural fabrication
  • NDT inspection coordination and quality documentation

Mechanical Engineering

Our mechanical team supports plant operators through planned shutdowns, breakdowns, and new installations. We supply qualified mechanical artisans, tradespeople, and engineers who integrate seamlessly into client operations to ensure minimal downtime and maximum plant availability.

  • Plant and equipment installation and commissioning
  • Planned maintenance and shutdown support
  • Mechanical equipment repair and refurbishment
  • Conveyor systems, drives, and rotating equipment
  • Skilled labour supply: fitters, boilermakers, artisans
  • Smelter, processing plant, and industrial facility works
  • Alignment, balancing, and vibration analysis support

Piping Systems

Access Engineering delivers full-scope piping solutions, from HDPE pipeline fabrication and underground installation to complex industrial piping modifications. We are ASME VIII and CEN/EN compliant, ensuring all pressure systems meet international code requirements.

  • HDPE pipeline fabrication and installation
  • Surface and underground piping systems
  • Industrial process piping modifications
  • Pipeline pressure testing and commissioning
  • Pipe spooling and fabrication in workshop
  • ASME VIII / CEN / EN code compliance
  • Maintenance and remedial piping works

Plate Works Fabrication

Our fabrication workshop is equipped to handle heavy-duty plate works and custom steel fabrication. With a 2% weld reject rate and ISO 3834 accreditation, we consistently deliver fabricated components and assemblies that meet the most demanding quality specifications in the mining and industrial sectors.

  • Storage tanks, vessels, and pressure equipment
  • Hoppers, chutes, skips, and conveyor components
  • Heavy-duty steel fabrication and rolling
  • Custom industrial components to client specifications
  • Workshop and on-site fabrication capability
  • Weld procedure qualification and NDT testing
  • ISO 3834 compliant quality management system

Rigging & Lifting

Rigging and lifting is our founding service and core specialisation. Our team of qualified riggers and lifting supervisors manage complex heavy lifts, plant relocations, and equipment installations in constrained industrial environments, with a zero-incident safety record as the benchmark.

  • Heavy lift planning, engineering, and execution
  • Plant and equipment relocation and installation
  • Complete plant dismantling and re-erection
  • Crane coordination and lifting equipment management
  • Lift study, rigging drawings, and methodology
  • Confined space and elevated rigging operations
  • Certified riggers and lifting supervisors on site
