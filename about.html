Company Overview

Engineering

Access Engineering Technology CC is a 100% Namibian-owned company specialising in Lifting & Rigging Engineering Services and a broad range of industrial engineering solutions. Established in 2014 and headquartered in Walvis Bay, Namibia, we have grown into one of the country's most trusted multi-discipline engineering contractors.

Our team of qualified and experienced engineers, technicians, and artisans delivers safe, innovative, and cost-effective solutions to the mining, construction, industrial, oil & gas, and energy sectors. We combine technical expertise, advanced equipment, and strict safety standards to deliver projects on time and within budget, every time.

We are proud holders of NOSCAR 5 Star Safety Rating, ISO 9001, ISO 45001:2018, and ISO 3834 accreditations, reflecting our unwavering commitment to quality and workplace safety. Our industry-leading 2% weld reject rate is a testament to the precision and skill of our fabrication team.

Our Core Values

Safety

Safety is non-negotiable. We hold a NOSCAR 5 Star rating and operate strict HSE management systems on every project site, protecting our people and the communities we work in.
